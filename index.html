Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is We-Deshi?

We-Deshi is a digital marketplace that promotes local businesses and products. We aim to connect buyers with sellers from their local communities, fostering economic growth and supporting small-scale enterprises.

Question 2: How can I buy products on We-Deshi?

To buy products on We-Deshi, simply browse through our listings, add items to your shopping cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay securely using various payment methods available on our platform.

Question 3: Can I sell my products on We-Deshi?

Yes, We-Deshi welcomes local sellers to join our platform and showcase their products. Simply sign up as a seller, list your products, and start reaching out to potential buyers in your community.
